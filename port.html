About me

I design the way I used to
build product lines —
obsessively, from the user out.

Before I was a UX designer, I was a merchandiser at Creative Pelt Pvt. Ltd., managing end-to-end product development for European and US fashion clients. I translated client briefs into physical products — which taught me more about user needs, material constraints, and iterative problem-solving than any course could.

Transitioning to digital design wasn't a leap — it was a translation. The research, the empathy, the back-and-forth with stakeholders: it all carries over. The screen is just a different material.

I hold a B.Des in Leather Goods from FDDI Kolkata, and I'm based in Patna. I'm looking for an internship or junior UI/UX role where I can contribute immediately and keep growing.
